Privacy Policy

We collect nothing.

Last updated: June 21, 2026

TabVault stores your sessions entirely inside Chrome's local storage on your own device. No data is ever transmitted to any server. The developer has zero access to your sessions, tabs, or browsing activity.

1. Overview

TabVault is a Chrome extension that saves, searches and restores browser sessions locally. This Privacy Policy explains what the extension accesses, how that information is used, and confirms that no personal data is ever collected or transmitted off your device.

By installing TabVault you agree to this policy.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

TabVault does not collect, transmit, store on any server, or share any of the following:

Personally identifiable information (name, email, address, phone)
Browsing history sent to any external server
Authentication or login credentials
Financial or payment information
Health information of any kind
Location data
Usage analytics, telemetry or crash reports
Personal communications or page content

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

To provide its core features, TabVault writes the following data locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage APIs. This data is never transmitted anywhere.

  • Session data — the URLs and titles of tabs you choose to save, stored in chrome.storage.local
  • Tags and preferences — custom tag names, colours, theme choice (dark/light), and onboarding state, stored in chrome.storage.local
  • Auto-save snapshots — a periodic snapshot of open tabs used for crash recovery, stored in chrome.storage.local
  • Window-close cache — a short-lived record of recently closed windows for one-click recovery, stored in chrome.storage.session and cleared automatically when the browser closes

The developer has no mechanism to access, read or retrieve any of this data. It exists solely on your machine.

4. Permissions Explained

TabVault requests only the permissions strictly necessary for its features.

PermissionWhy it is needed
tabsRead tab URLs and titles when you save a session. No data leaves the device.
storageSave sessions, tags and preferences locally using Chrome's built-in storage.
windowsDetect window close events to offer the one-click recovery banner.
alarmsSchedule the 30-minute background auto-save. No network requests are made.
contextMenusAdd a "Save session" option to your right-click context menu.
tabGroupsRead Chrome tab group names when saving so they can be restored later.

5. Third-Party Services

TabVault uses no third-party analytics, advertising networks, cloud storage providers, crash-reporting services or any other external service. Zero data is shared with any third party under any circumstances.

6. Children's Privacy

TabVault does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The extension requires no account, no sign-up, and transmits no data — making it safe for users of all ages.
